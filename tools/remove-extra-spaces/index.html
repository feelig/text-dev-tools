How it works

This tool finds repeated spaces in your text and reduces them to single spaces. It helps clean messy copy before you paste it into forms, editors, spreadsheets, websites, or AI prompts.

It keeps normal line structure while improving spacing quality, so your text becomes easier to read and more consistent.
